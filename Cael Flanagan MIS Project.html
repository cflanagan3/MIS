Home

I'm from the town of East Wenatchee, Washington. The population is 14,236 making it the biggest city in Douglas County. Fun Fact: East Wenatchee was the landing site of the first nonstop flight across the Pacific Ocean on October 5, 1931. The flight was completed by Clyde Pangborn and his journey began in Misawa, Japan. To commemorate this event, the cities of East Wenatchee and Misawa formed a sister city relationship.

Hobbies

Some hobbies I enjoy include hiking, playing basketball, and playing piano. I also enjoy playing basketball; whether it's playing with friends or just hooping by myself, basketball helps me block out all distractions and just enjoy life.
